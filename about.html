Data driven. Future focused.

Orchid A+ builds practical tools at the intersection of data, AI, and everyday decisions — starting with health risk screening, and expanding from there.

Why we started

Most data science projects stop at a Jupyter notebook. We wanted to see what happens when a model actually gets put in front of real people — not as a polished, overconfident black box, but as a tool that's honest about what it knows and doesn't know.

Health Check and Risk Rewind grew out of exactly that question: could a modest cardiovascular dataset become something genuinely useful, without overselling what it can predict?

What we're building toward

Orchid A+ is expanding beyond health tools into a wider set of practical, data-driven products — automation, AI content creation, and forecasting tools — all built with the same approach: transparent about limitations, useful in practice.

Check the Our Products page for what's live now, and what's coming next.
